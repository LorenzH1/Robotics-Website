The Robot

This is our robot, the Ruumba. It uses Pneumatics to apply air pressure
so that the clamp can clamp down upon mobile goals,
then it will lift them up through a 1:3 gear ratio, making it easy for us to move them around the rink.
Our main goal is to bring back neutral goals to our designated allaince side
or lifting them up onto the alliance platform to score more points.
We also plan to create a system that is similar to a conveyer where it will transfer
mogos down a schute of plexi glass down to a a mobile goal that we have in our possession.
Our Automnous can work both on the right and left side of the rink,
it's main function for both is to try to launch rings via accleration into a mobile goal that is in front of the robot.
The other function that works only on the left side can not only do the main function,
but it will then move toward a neutral goal and clamp down upon to move it
back behind the line that tells if it is truly on our side
to gain both the win point and a further advantage in driver period.
