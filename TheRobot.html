This is our robot:
the Ruumba.


Main Function:

It uses Pneumatics to apply air pressure so that the clamp can clamp down upon mobile goals, then it will lift them up through a 1:3 gear ratio, making it easy for us to move them around the rink.

Main Goal:

Our main goal is to bring back neutral goals to our designated allaince side or lifting them up onto the alliance platform to score more points.


Future Plans:

We also plan to create a system that is similar to a conveyer where it will transfer rings down a schute of plexi glass to a mobile goal that we have clamped upon.

Automnous Programs:

Our Automnous can work both on the right and left side of the rink, it's main function for both is to try to launch rings via accleration into a mobile goal that is in front of the robot. The other function that works only on the left side can not only do the main function, but it will then move toward a neutral goal and clamp down upon to move it back behind the line that tells if it is truly on our side to gain both the win point and a further advantage in driver period.
